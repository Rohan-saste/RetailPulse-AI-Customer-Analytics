### Slide 2: The Business Case
- **The Problem:** Retailers lose billions of dollars annually due to stock misallocations and customer attrition.
- **The Solution:** RetailPulse compiles transactional data, segments customers, predicts future demand, and alerts on inventory risk.
- **Quantified Targets:**
  - Reduce stockouts by 30-50%.
  - Raise revenue by 15-25% through optimized logistics.

### Slide 3: Core Functional Capabilities
- **F-01: Data Ingestion** - Sanitized transactional history containing 1M+ records.
- **F-02: Customer Segmentation** - Behavioral cluster analysis using RFM profiles.
- **F-03: Demand Forecasting** - Forward-looking sales predictions using Prophet.
- **F-04: Churn Prediction** - Interactive classification playground mapping churn risks.
- **F-05: Inventory Health** - Data-driven Reorder Points (ROP) & Safety Stock calculations.

### Slide 5: Customer Segmentation Highlights
- **K-Means Centroids:** Separated database into Premium, Loyal, Regular, and At Risk groups.
- **Visual Mapping:** Displayed using interactive 3D spatial scatter plots mapping Recency, Frequency, and Monetary scores.
- **Strategic Focus:** Focus win-back campaigns on the At Risk segment representing 34% of active users.

### Slide 6: Demand Forecasting & Seasonality
- **Model Performance:** Achieved 11.2% MAPE on rolling daily test splits.
- **Projections:** Generates forecast timelines for the next 90 days.
- **Seasonality Findings:** Peak sales occur in Nov-Dec holiday periods, presenting warehouse stock challenges.

### Slide 7: Churn Simulator & Observability
